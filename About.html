JAYDEEP KOTADIYA Ceramic Art Teacher

About Owner

A PASSIONATE CREATOR

Mr. Jaydeep Kotadiya, an accomplished Ceramic Art teacher, currently holds a position
in the sculpture department at Surat College of Fine Art, VNSGU.< He earned his bachelor's
degree in sculpture and completed his master's in Ceramic from Santiniketan, West Bengal,
in 2018.Since then, he has showcased his expertise through various art exhibitions and
actively participated in workshops.

With a rich academic background, Mr. Kotadiya holds a Master of Fine Arts (M.F.A.) in
Ceramic & Glass from Vishwa Bharti University, Santiniketan. Bringing his passion for
ceramic art into education, he has been contributing to the field since 2019.
For the past two years, he has served as a Teaching Assistant at Surat College of Fine Art,
where he imparts his knowledge and skills to aspiring artists.

About Studio

A STUDIO WHICH SAVES YOUR MEMORY

In addition to his academic role, Mr. Kotadiya is the proud owner of Mudberry
Pottery Studio, where he combines his teaching experience with hands-on artistry.
Through the studio, he extends his artistic vision to the community, offering a
space for creativity and learning. With a dedication to both education and
art practice, Mr. Jaydeep Kotadiya continues to inspire and contribute to the
world of ceramic art.
